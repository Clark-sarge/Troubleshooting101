Handling Panic

When pressure mounts, focus on the mantra. Take a break, ask for help, or try a different narrowing tactic. The goal is to shift from worrying about consequences to taking a small, logical next step.

Handling Anger

Frustration impairs judgment and can lead to mistakes or damage. If you feel angry, step away. A short walk can reset your perspective and prevent costly errors.
